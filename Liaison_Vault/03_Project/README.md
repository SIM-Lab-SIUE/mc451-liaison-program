# \ud83c\udfaf Project - Your Active Research Work

This is your **command center**\u2014everything related to your semester research project lives here.

## Folder Structure

```
03_Project/
\u251c\u2500\u2500 01_Prospectus/     \u2190 Research questions & project brief (Week 6)
\u251c\u2500\u2500 02_Codebook/       \u2190 Variable definitions & coding rules (Week 10)
\u251c\u2500\u2500 03_Data/           \u2190 Raw data, CSV files, analysis notes
\u2514\u2500\u2500 04_Drafts/         \u2190 Report drafts, visualizations, writing iterations
```

---\n\n## \ud83d\udcdd 01_Prospectus - Your Research Design\n\n**Due:** Week 6 (End of Phase II: The Architect)\n\n### What Goes Here\n- Your research questions\n- Literature review summary\n- Proposed methodology\n- Sample description\n- Timeline\n\n### Key Documents\n- `Research_Questions.md` - Your RQs and hypotheses\n- `Literature_Summary.md` - Synthesis of key theories\n- `Methods_Plan.md` - How you'll collect/analyze data\n- `Project_Timeline.md` - Week-by-week plan\n\n---\n\n## \ud83d\udcd6 02_Codebook - Your Data Dictionary\n\n**Due:** Week 10 (Phase III: The Translator)\n\n### What Goes Here\n- Data dictionary (use the template!)\n- Coding rules and decision log\n- Intercoder reliability testing notes\n- Pilot test results\n\n### Key Documents\n- `Data_Dictionary.md` - Copy from `99_Templates/Data_Dictionary_Template.md`\n- `Coding_Rules.md` - Edge cases and difficult decisions\n- `Reliability_Test.md` - Intercoder agreement notes\n\n**This is THE most important document for content analysis projects.**\n\n---\n\n## \ud83d\udccb 03_Data - Your Raw Materials\n\n**Active:** Weeks 11-15 (Phase IV: The Analyst)\n\n### What Goes Here\n- Raw CSV files\n- Data cleaning notes\n- R scripts for analysis\n- Intermediate datasets\n- Analysis logs\n\n### Recommended Structure\n```\n03_Data/\n\u251c\u2500\u2500 raw/              \u2190 Original, untouched data\n\u251c\u2500\u2500 clean/            \u2190 Processed, ready-to-analyze data\n\u251c\u2500\u2500 scripts/          \u2190 R code for cleaning/analysis\n\u2514\u2500\u2500 analysis_log.md   \u2190 Notes on what you tried\n```\n\n### Data Management Rules\n1. **Never edit raw data** - Always work on copies\n2. **Document everything** - Future-you needs to know what you did\n3. **Version your files** - `data_v1.csv`, `data_v2_clean.csv`\n4. **Back up regularly** - Data loss is tragedy\n\n---\n\n## \u270f\ufe0f 04_Drafts - Your Writing Workshop\n\n**Active:** Weeks 14-17 (Phase IV & V: Analyst \u2192 Publisher)\n\n### What Goes Here\n- Report drafts (Quarto `.qmd` files)\n- Visualizations (charts, graphs)\n- Presentation slides\n- Peer review feedback\n- Final portfolio materials\n\n### Recommended Structure\n```\n04_Drafts/\n\u251c\u2500\u2500 report_v1.qmd      \u2190 First draft\n\u251c\u2500\u2500 report_v2.qmd      \u2190 After peer review\n\u251c\u2500\u2500 report_final.qmd   \u2190 Submission version\n\u251c\u2500\u2500 figures/           \u2190 All your ggplot2 outputs\n\u251c\u2500\u2500 tables/            \u2190 Formatted tables\n\u2514\u2500\u2500 peer_feedback.md   \u2190 Notes from classmates\n```\n\n---\n\n## Project Timeline (Semester Overview)\n\n| Week | Phase | Folder Focus | Deliverable |\n|------|-------|-------------|-------------|\n| 1-3 | Journalist | Setup | Journal entries |\n| 4-6 | Architect | `01_Prospectus/` | **Project brief due** |\n| 7-10 | Translator | `02_Codebook/` | **Codebook due** |\n| 11-15 | Analyst | `03_Data/` | Data analysis |\n| 16-17 | Publisher | `04_Drafts/` | **Final report due** |\n\n---\n\n## Tips for Success\n\n### 1. Work in Public\nShare your prospectus and codebook drafts with classmates early. Feedback saves time.\n\n### 2. Version Control Your Drafts\nDon't overwrite! Keep `v1`, `v2`, `v3` versions. You might need to reference old ideas.\n\n### 3. Link Everything\nYour project notes should connect to:\n- `[[Literature notes]]`\n- `[[Weekly journals]]`\n- `[[Data dictionary]]`\n\n### 4. Back Up Obsessively\nUse:\n- OneDrive/Dropbox/Google Drive\n- GitHub (even better!)\n- External hard drive\n\n**You don't have cloud backup until you have 3 copies in 2 locations.**\n\n---\n\n## Common Pitfalls to Avoid\n\n\u274c **Starting data collection before your codebook is done**\n\u2192 You'll have to recode everything\n\n\u274c **Not pilot testing your variables**\n\u2192 You'll discover problems too late\n\n\u274c **Waiting until Week 16 to start writing**\n\u2192 You'll panic and produce mediocre work\n\n\u274c **Not documenting your decisions**\n\u2192 You'll forget why you made key choices\n\n---\n\n## Example: A Well-Organized Project Folder\n\n```\n03_Project/\n\u251c\u2500\u2500 01_Prospectus/\n\u2502   \u251c\u2500\u2500 Research_Questions.md\n\u2502   \u251c\u2500\u2500 Literature_Summary.md\n\u2502   \u251c\u2500\u2500 Methods_Plan.md\n\u2502   \u2514\u2500\u2500 Project_Timeline.md\n\u251c\u2500\u2500 02_Codebook/\n\u2502   \u251c\u2500\u2500 Data_Dictionary.md\n\u2502   \u251c\u2500\u2500 Coding_Rules.md\n\u2502   \u251c\u2500\u2500 Pilot_Test_Notes.md\n\u2502   \u2514\u2500\u2500 Reliability_Test.md\n\u251c\u2500\u2500 03_Data/\n\u2502   \u251c\u2500\u2500 raw/\n\u2502   \u2502   \u2514\u2500\u2500 original_data.csv\n\u2502   \u251c\u2500\u2500 clean/\n\u2502   \u2502   \u2514\u2500\u2500 cleaned_data.csv\n\u2502   \u251c\u2500\u2500 scripts/\n\u2502   \u2502   \u251c\u2500\u2500 01_import.R\n\u2502   \u2502   \u251c\u2500\u2500 02_clean.R\n\u2502   \u2502   \u2514\u2500\u2500 03_analyze.R\n\u2502   \u2514\u2500\u2500 analysis_log.md\n\u2514\u2500\u2500 04_Drafts/\n    \u251c\u2500\u2500 report_v1.qmd\n    \u251c\u2500\u2500 report_v2_peer_review.qmd\n    \u251c\u2500\u2500 report_final.qmd\n    \u251c\u2500\u2500 figures/\n    \u2502   \u251c\u2500\u2500 chart_1.png\n    \u2502   \u2514\u2500\u2500 chart_2.png\n    \u2514\u2500\u2500 peer_feedback.md\n```\n\n---\n\n**This is where the magic happens.
